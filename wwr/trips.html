Easy Rapids Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this half-day trip takes you down gentle Class I-II rapids through breathtaking scenery. Enjoy calm waters, splashing fun, and time to relax along the riverbanks.

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Easy

Canyon Rush

Step up the excitement with this full-day trip featuring Class III-IV rapids through rugged canyon terrain. A thrilling adventure for those ready to paddle hard and laugh harder!

Duration: 6 hours | Difficulty: Moderate

Extreme Rapids Expedition

For the true adrenaline junkies! Conquer wild Class IV-V rapids with our expert guides. Not for the faint of heart — this two-day expedition includes camping under the stars beside the roaring river.

Duration: 2 days | Difficulty: Advanced
